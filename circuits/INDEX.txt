for now (schematics / KiCad sheets come later when you draw them).

04_p1_5v_current_limit      Meter +5V to RJ12 pin1 (limited)

06_rj12_connector           Female 6P6C pinout
07_data_request_sense       Pin2 → GPIO
08_open_collector_data      UART TX → pin5 OC
09_esd_protection           TVS on USB + RJ12
10_ui_led_button            LED + button
11_optional_isolation       Optos / iso DC-DC (skip for v1)

Suggested buy-list shortcut for a lab prototype
-----------------------------------------------
- ESP32-C3-MINI-1
- USB-C jack + USBLC6-2SC6
- Adafruit PowerBoost 1000C (+ LiPo) OR USB_5V wired straight to SYS_5V
- 3.3 V buck module or SY8088
- TI TPS2595 (or AP2553) for P1_5V limit
- RJ12 6P6C jack
- BSS138 + 4.7k + divider 2x10k
- LED + switch
