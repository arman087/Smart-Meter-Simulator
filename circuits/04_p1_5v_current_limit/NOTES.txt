04 — P1 +5 V CURRENT LIMIT — DROPPED
====================================

STATUS
------
**Removed from this project.** Lab simulator only — do not design an
eFuse / current-limit IC for RJ12 pin 1.

WIRING INSTEAD
--------------
  Lipo Rider Plus header 5V  →  SYS_5V / P1_5V  →  RJ12 pin 1

Optional (not required): a bulk cap (e.g. 47–100 µF) near RJ12 pin 1
for Ghost inrush. That is decoupling, not a current limiter.

DSMR meters use ~250 mA continuous; we intentionally skip matching that
hardware limit to keep the board simple.
