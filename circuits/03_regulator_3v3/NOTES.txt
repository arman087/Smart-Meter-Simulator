03 — 3.3 V REGULATOR
===================

STATUS WITH LIPO RIDER PLUS
---------------------------
**Skip this block** if you mount Seeed Lipo Rider Plus and use its
header **3V3** pin for the ESP32 (see circuit 02 + mechanicals/).

The Lipo Rider 3V3 rail is ~250 mA. That is enough for ESP32-C3 Wi‑Fi
in this lab use. Put ceramics next to the ESP32 module anyway.

WHEN YOU STILL NEED THIS BLOCK
------------------------------
Only if:
  - you run desk-only from board USB 5V with no Lipo Rider, or
  - Wi‑Fi brownouts on the module 3V3 and you want a stronger buck
    from SYS_5V (e.g. SY8088 / AP2112-3.3).

Then: SYS_5V → 3.3 V regulator → VCC_3V3, caps near ESP32.

TEST
----
VCC_3V3 = 3.3 V ±3%. Under Wi‑Fi TX, dips stay above ~3.0 V.
