02 — SEEED LIPO RIDER PLUS (POWER DAUGHTERBOARD)
===============================================

Buy: Seeed Lipo Rider Plus (SKU 106990290)
Wiki: https://wiki.seeedstudio.com/Lipo-Rider-Plus/

ROLE
----
Finished module. Do not design charger/boost on the main PCB.

  USB-C on module  → charge LiPo (power only)
  Header 5V        → SYS_5V
  Header 3V3       → ESP32 VCC
  JST              → 1S LiPo ≥ 1000 mAh (protected)
  USB-A on module  → ignore

ESP32 flash/serial uses the **main board** USB-C (circuit 01) with D+/D−.
This module does not expose USB data to the ESP32.

HEADER PIN ORDER (J3)
---------------------
1 USB | 2 GND | 3 BAT | 4 GND | 5 5V | 6 GND | 7 EN | 8 3V3

EN to GND turns 5V output off. Leave open for normal use.
