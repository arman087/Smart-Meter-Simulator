02 — SEEED LIPO RIDER PLUS (DAUGHTERBOARD)
=========================================

CHOSEN SOLUTION
---------------
Seeed Lipo Rider Plus — charger/booster made for MCU projects.
Use as a **daughterboard / sidecar** next to (or stacked on) the main PCB.
You draw the footprint yourself (or use mechanicals/ as a starting point).

Wiki: https://wiki.seeedstudio.com/Lipo-Rider-Plus/
SKU:  106990290

WHAT IT GIVES
-------------
- USB-C → charge 1S LiPo
- JST → battery
- Header **5V**  → SYS_5V → RJ12 pin 1
- Header **3V3** → ESP32 VCC
- GND
- USB-A → ignore

USB charge unplugged + battery connected → **5 V and 3.3 V still on**.

HEADER (J3) ORDER
-----------------
1 USB | 2 GND | 3 BAT | 4 GND | 5 5V | 6 GND | 7 EN | 8 3V3

EN → GND cuts 5V output. Leave open for normal use.

MAIN PCB
--------
- Pads / header matching Lipo Rider (your footprint)
- Board USB-C for ESP32 D+/D− only (circuit 01)
- No on-board charger, no second 3.3 V regulator, no P1 current-limit IC

MECHANICALS

TEST
----
Battery only: 5V + 3V3 present.
USB-C on Lipo Rider: charges; rails stay up.
Unplug USB-C: still running from LiPo.
Ghost on RJ12 gets 5V.
